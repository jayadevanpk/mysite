Sobriety Tracker (Chrome Extension)

Sobriety Tracker is a minimalist Chrome extension that helps you stay committed to your no-drinking goals. It shows your progress since the last drink, surfaces motivational health milestones, and includes a reset button. Built for privacy and simplicity.

Spend Mr Ambani's Money(Chrome Extension)

Ambani Project is an interactive experiment that lets you try spending Mukesh Ambani’s fortune. It’s a playful way to understand just how hard it is to visualise extreme wealth — and how impossible it is to run out of digits.
